## In this preliminary upload I am simply showcasing basic data cleaning techniques. 🛠️

- I have added color to the headers for distinction and made the headers in bold

- Then I selected all the columns and double-clicked on the vertical line separating them to induce even space among all. 

- Then I fixed spelling of words like - 'American Expres' to 'American Express' by selecting the particular row(M). 

- Then the empty rows under Customer name(A) were deleted, as it is a customer demographic, without the customer name it is useless. This was done by selecting the entire dataset and applying filters to the entirety. Then under column A the 'blanks' were removed. Finally the remaining empty rows were deleted successfully.

- Then duplicate values under the Order Id was removed with the help of `conditional formatting`. Highlighting the duplicate order Ids with red and then removing them from the dataset.

- Misspelling and incorrect iteration of the word 'JCB' was fixed next with the help of `Find & Replace` option.

- Finally the date format was also changed as per my liking, by choosing from different options under the `Number Format` ribbon.
